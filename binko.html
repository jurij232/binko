About Binko

Binko predicts waste type from a photo and helps you sort it into the correct bin.

It recognises plastic, paper, glass, metal, organic waste, batteries, clothes, and general waste.

Includes a map with nearby recycling locations and a history of previous scans.

Predictions are not always 100% accurate. Always double-check important waste before disposal.
